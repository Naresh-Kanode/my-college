Arohan is a welcome party for new entrants in MGM College. It gives and greats plesure to welcome our newly admitted students with a great enjoyments. So, every year our college organizes fresher's party. i.e. Arohan. In this party student give various performance like solo & group dance , solo & group signing etc. In this party MGM conducts Mr. Fresher's & Miss Fresher's competition in which students are tested on the basis of their talents & excellence in different contests like introduction , questions and answers. This competition judged by Dr.Mrs Geeta Lathkar, Prof.S.L. Kotgire, and senior staff members.
