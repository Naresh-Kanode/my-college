Department of Computer Science and Information Technology

In every part of life, use of computers becomes essential. Right from railway reservati to sophisticated medical equipments, computers are inevitable. In every field, there is high demand of computer professionals. In modern world of Information Technology, to achieve the top position, one must have to be updated with recent knowledge like VB.NET, Neural Network, VC++, JAVA etc. The College provides M.Sc. Software Engineering course that gives the deep and current knowledge to satisfy the need of industry. Students are prepared to adopt these high technologies by getting all basic and advance knowledge, which is covered in the graduation course of B.Sc. Computer Science. Computer knowledge is not restricted to the science students; rather students of Arts, Commerce and MCVC are also having good opportunities to make their careers in computers. The BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) course, which is basically designed for them, provides equal opportunities as like engineering and science students to get good job. Computer and IT is the leading industry that provides more jobs and opportunities with luxuries and high salaries in India as well as in abroad.
