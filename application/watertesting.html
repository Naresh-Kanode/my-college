Water Analysis unit


This water analysis unit was founded in the November 2011, from the inspiration of our Hon'ble chairman K.K. Kadam for awareness and welfare of society. This unit mainly conducts sixteen different tests which includes from physical, chemical and biological prospective. In the physical examination four tests are carried out like smell, turbidity, etc. In the chemical examination of water presence of different salts are check out which mainly includes presence of Calcium, Nitrate, Sulphate, Copper, Manganese, Magnesium etc and the presence of hardness, salinity etc. The most important part of this unit is the biological examination of water in which Most Probable Number (MPN), presence of Thermo stability, presence of pathogens like Salmonella typhi, Vibrio species, Shigella Sp. were carried out..
