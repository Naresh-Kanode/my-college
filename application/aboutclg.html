Students from all walks of life across the country are able to learn and get educated in more than 50 Institutions run by the Trust across its five centers in India. Dedication to social service has also been another important objective of MGM Trust and keeping in line with the same, the Trust has been advocating rural education. It has also been a frontrunner in realizing and imbibing the importance of environment. In fact, MGM Trust has developed an entire park known as the Priyadarshini Udyan in Aurangabad. Efforts for GREEN AURANGABAD have been lauded by the United Nations and the Trust Secretary; Mr. Ankushrao Kadam has also been awarded and felicitated by the UN for the Environment Friendly Approach.

ACHIEVEMENTS

1 MGM has established AIDS Research Centre in collaboration with Texas University, USA,with all latest equipments for diagnosis, treatment and research. It also has academic linkages with ICMR and AIIMS, New Delhi.
2 MGM offers distance education programs in collaboration with IIT, Mumbai, wherein online interactive lectures of experts from IIT are arranged through satellite linkage

4 An agreement for academic collaboration with Pittsburgh State University, USA, has been signed.

7 Training collaboration with IBM, Oracle and Microsoft.
8 Till date MGM has sponsored more than 25 faculty members for their doctoral work and more than 150 for post-graduation.
9 MGM liberally sends its faculty members to attend national and international conferences and seminars, to keep the faculty abreast with the latest technological advancement.
10 MGM proudly mentions 25 awards and honors along with more than 600 paper publications. by faculty at national and international levels. Many players have won the prizes in inter-university and national level competitions in individual and team events

Medical Education

1 Medical colleges have state of the art facilities, for under-graduate and post-graduate facility with exclusive health care services like emergency management system, fertility and test-tube baby center, advanced endoscopy and laparoscopy eye-care center. More than 60,000 people from tribal and rural areas of Marathwada have been treated free of cost. Mother NGO status has been awarded by the Health Ministry of India. Recently we have been awarded University Status by University Grants Commission and HRD Ministry for 2 medical colleges under Section 3 of UGC Act. Thus, MGM University of Health Sciences has come into existence and the University has framed its own Rules and Regulations for its functioning in keeping with the objectives of the institution.
