Courses Offered

Bachelor of Computer Science (B.Sc.(CS))is a 3 years under-graduate course with a specialization in the domain of computer science, software and hardware related aspects. The course is a bachelors level program in the area of computer science which encompasses important areas like programming, software, hardware, and networks under its ambit. The motive of the course is to provide students sufficient knowledge and skills that would be effective enough to help them channelize their learning in a practical sense.
